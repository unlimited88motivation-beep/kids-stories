How to Make Kids Sleep Fast (Simple Guide)

Many parents struggle to help their kids fall asleep quickly. The good news is that simple habits can make a big difference.

1. Create a Calm Environment

Turn off lights, reduce noise, and make the bedroom comfortable.

2. Avoid Screens Before Bed

Screens keep the brain active and delay sleep.

3. Use Bedtime Stories

Calming stories are one of the best tools to help kids relax and sleep faster.

Try a Sleep Story 🌙

4. Stay Consistent

Use the same routine every night to train the brain.

Conclusion

With consistency and the right routine, your child will fall asleep faster and sleep better.
