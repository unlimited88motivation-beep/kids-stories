Why Kids Fear the Dark

Fear of the dark is common in children and can affect their sleep.

Why It Happens

  • Active imagination
  • Lack of control
  • Fear of unknown

How to Help

  • Use a soft light
  • Stay calm and supportive
  • Use calming bedtime stories
Reduce Fear Now

Final Thoughts

With patience and the right tools, children can overcome fear and sleep peacefully.
