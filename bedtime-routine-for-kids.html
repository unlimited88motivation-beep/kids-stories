Best Bedtime Routine for Kids

A good bedtime routine helps children feel safe and relaxed before sleep.

Step 1: Set a Fixed Time

Going to bed at the same time every night improves sleep quality.

Step 2: Calm Activities

Reading a story is better than playing or watching screens.

Read a Relaxing Story

Step 3: Create Comfort

Use dim lights and a quiet environment.

Step 4: Repeat Daily

Consistency is the key to success.
